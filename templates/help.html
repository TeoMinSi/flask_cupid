Q1. Is this a real-time dashboard? No. Currently, the dashboard is relying on an auto-scheduled refresh up to 8 times per day.
Q2. Is sentiment analysis being conducted real-time? Yes. The sentiment analysis will trigger the real-time scraping of tweets on each merchant (for the most recent week) and conduct sentiment scoring.
Q3. What’s the difference between Upload Data and Data Prediction Form? Upload Data serves to populate dashboards with the bank’s survey data, while the Data Prediction Form aims to predict a new customer’s categories of interest, RFM segment and preferred merchants given their demographics details.
